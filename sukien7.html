MAY MEMBERSHIP SWAP

April 28, 2019

Date/Time
May 1, 2019 - May 31, 2019
12:00 am

Location
CENTRAL Zoo


Content
The CENTRAL Zoo and The Mariners’ Museum and Park are swapping members for the month of May.CENTRAL Zoo members who visit The Mariners’ Museum will enjoy free admission for up to 6 people each day, a 10% discount in the gift shop, and $1 off movie tickets. Not valid for special events.Members of the CENTRAL Zoo that also become members of The Mariners’ Museum during the month of May, can take advantage of one additional month on Mariners’ Museum memberships.
