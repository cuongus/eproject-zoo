Welcome to Central Zoo!

Central Zoo is England’s oldest zoological garden and home to the world’s largest variety of species. Almost 20,000 animals of around 1,300 species live in the 33-hectare zoo. They include elephants, giraffes, gorillas, and England’s only giant pandas.
